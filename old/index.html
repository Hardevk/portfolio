Key assignment I completed my first-year at Laurier.


  • As part of a team project in the bachelor of UXD first year, we were tasked with improving the https://www.isow.ca/ website. ISOW (International Students Overcoming War) aims to help international students with their education in Canada. Some of the Issues with their webpage were poor site flow, unclear structure, and missing calls to action. These pain points were rectified in the revised journey map. The pain points were also validated by interviews conducted in person. Based on the pain points identified, a new wireframe (using Figma) for the https://www.isow.ca/ website was created.
